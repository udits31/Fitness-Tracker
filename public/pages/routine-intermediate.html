Intermediate Push/Pull/Legs

Intermediate Gain Muscle Gym 3 Routines

Push Day

Focuses on the push muscles: chest, shoulders, and triceps.

  • Warm Up 1 set
  • Bench Press (Barbell) 6 sets · 6-10 reps
  • Incline Bench Press (Dumbbell) 4 sets · 10-12 reps
  • Seated Shoulder Press (Machine) 4 sets · 10-12 reps
  • Cable Fly Crossovers 4 sets · 12-15 reps
  • Lateral Raise (Dumbbell) 3 sets · 12-15 reps
  • Triceps Rope Pushdown 3 sets · 15-20 reps

Pull Day

Focuses on the pull muscles: back and biceps.

  • Warm Up 1 set
  • Bent Over Row (Barbell) 6 sets · 6-10 reps
  • Inverted Row 4 sets
  • Lat Pulldown (Cable) 4 sets · 10-12 reps
  • Shrug (Dumbbell) 4 sets · 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curl (Barbell) 4 sets · 12-15 reps
  • Face Pull 3 sets · 15-20 reps

Legs Day

Focuses on the lower body: quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves.

  • Warm Up 1 set
  • Squat (Barbell) 6 sets · 6-10 reps
  • Leg Press (Machine) 4 sets · 10-12 reps
  • Glute Ham Raise 4 sets
  • Lying Leg Curl (Machine) 4 sets · 12-15 reps
  • Leg Extension (Machine) 3 sets · 12-15 reps
  • Standing Calf Raise (Machine) 4 sets · 12-15 reps
